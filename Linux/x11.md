`xauth` -> it manages authentication cookies (magic cookies) in `~/.Xauthority` for secure X11 server connections, especially via SSH-x/-Y forwarding

These cookies (in `~/.Xauthority`) shown by `xauth list` are MIT-MAGIC-COOKIE-1 secrets.
They are random 128-bit keys that authenticate X11 clients to your X server/display, preventing unauthorised apps from drawing to/controlling your screen.
