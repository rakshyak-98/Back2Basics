A greedy algorithm is a problem-solving strategy that makes the best possible choice at each immediate step, with the hope that these local "best" choices will lead to the best overall (global) solution.

> [!NOTE]
> Take the best thing available right now
> - Choose the best available option from the current state.
> - Check if this choice is valid (does it follow the rules/constraints?).
> - If it's valid, add it to your solution and move to the next step.
