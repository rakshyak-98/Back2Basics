[[codec streams]]

A codec (coder-decoder) is an algorithm that compresses and decompresses digital audio or video data.

Encoding -> converts raw audio/video into a compressed format for storage or transmission.
Decoding -> reverses the process, converting compressed data back into playable audio/video

Two categories:
- **Video codecs**: H.264, HEVC (H.265), VP9, AV1, MPEG-2
- **Audio codecs**: AAC, MP3, AC-3, Opus, MP2
